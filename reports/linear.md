## The Intervention

First 30 days of AEO work with Linear would prioritize:

1. Own the 'Jira alternative' search surface with direct migration content — the 'switching from Jira' prompt is the highest-traffic entry point and Linear underperforms there.
1. Publish benchmark content on cycle time and engineering throughput that AI can cite — KPC framing is currently skewed toward 'clean UI' rather than velocity metrics.
1. Seed more enterprise proof points: AI models associate Linear with smaller teams, which creates ceiling risk as the company moves upmarket.
